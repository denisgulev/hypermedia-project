Work With Us


Career Opportunities

  • Disability Support Worker

    Work as part of team to ensure proper care of body mechanics, safety and the overall well-being of all clients.

  • Social Care Worker for Adults

    Train life skills: road safety, use of public transport, cooking
    Responsible for preparing meals, snacks and drinks for clients
    Communicate with care managers on behalf of clients
    Propose social events, appointments and places of worship for clients
    Assist clients on matters of personal care including bathing, dressing, eating and medication

  • Disability Support

    Assist clients that have a range of physical and intellectual disabilities
    Creating and implementing individual program plans to enable learning and independence
    Assisting clients with their day-to-day lives through meal prep, medication handling + personal care

  • Social Care Worker for Children

    Supervise a program which involves working with the local council and the department of human services to provide vacation care to both mainstream and disabled children
    Provide in home 1:1 support to disabled children
    Provide 1:1 and group support to disabled children on weekend recreational activities

Volunteering Opportunities

  • Administration Volunteer

    Both the Victim Services staff and the Education staff are always in need of general administrative support including database entry and filing.

  • Special Event Planning Committees

    Volunteers are encouraged to promote events, gather and organize items for auctions and other fundraising activities, and help with the planning and executing of events.

  • Child and Family Hospitality Volunteer

    The duties of a child hospitality volunteer are helping out our Client Relations Specialist/Receptionist with providing the snacks and coffee and really just being friendly to and chatting/playing with the families and children to help them relax and feel safe on what is most likely a very stressful and upsetting day for them. As a child and family hospitality volunteer, you will be encouraged to initiate crafts, organize the play room, restock the snack, drink and coffee stations, and do other small duties to provide support for our team.
