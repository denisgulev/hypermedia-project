Who We Are


Description

The Ariel Care center is a center dedicated for adults and young poeple with sever physical disabilities and mental health issues. It was founded in 1990 with the Milan center being the first one. Through the hard work of the staff and thanks to the generosity of the people, it was possible for the Ariel Care Center to grow over the years to have many other facilities located too in Lombardy, a region of Northern Italy.


Mission

The Ariel Care Center will provide excellence in program delivery, research driven practices and techniques, state-of-the-art technology and services for individuals and families impacted with the challenges of disabilities.
